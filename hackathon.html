Interdisciplinary Hackathon is:

A large collaborative event, open to all majors on campus, where students can join together in small teams and use their unique skills and strengths to work on projects related to issues of equity and social justice. This event is being funded by HESAC through the Husky Seed Fund Grant, thank you!

Our Schedule:

  • Virtual room opens 9:00-10:00

    Opening “ceremony”/talk/intro + reiterate tracks & prompt

  • Team Brainstorm 10:00-11:00

    Meet/chat with your team + start coming up with an idea for your project

  • Meet with mentor 11:00-12:00

    Meet the mentors (mentors go around introduce yourselves)

  • Lunck Break 12:00-1:00
  • Working 1:00-6:00

    Continue working, mentors cycle through occasionally

  • Dinner break 6:00-6:45
  • Symposium within each track 6:45-7:45
  • Each track vote on most creative/resourceful/other adjectives 7:45-8:00
  • Award announcements & Closing “ceremony” 8:00-8:30
